About us

We are the antidote to insurance. We can insure that you have as little insurance as you can handle.

How we got here

Our partners come from a wide variety of professional backgrounds. Our team includes experts in everything from arms dealing to performance acrobatics. We're supported by a dedicated legal unit that helps us take care of our customers without all the red tape.

Established in some of the most stable times in human history to bring back a bit of that old-fashioned sense of uncertainty. We endeavour to make your life as unpredictable as possible. Our range of unsurance plans will target the most predictable parts of your life and ensure an unstable outcome.

Where we're headed

We strive to create a more balanced world where instability and chaos are as well appreciated as stability. How can one enjoy such high levels of security without the ever-present promise of a surprise? How can one enjoy a warm Summer's day without knowing the cold? Our aim is to have a global impact on the excitement of being.
